Our Founders

Mr. Mike Smith || Mrs. Diana Smith

Mr. Mike Smith studied Education at the University of Cape Coast. He later trained in school administration and worked as a teacher for several years. He became a head teacher before starting the school. He focuses on discipline, planning, and strong academic standards.
His wife Mrs. Diana Smith studied Early Childhood Education at the University of Education, Winneba. She worked as a kindergarten teacher. She also trained other teachers in child development. She supports the lower primary department and manages student welfare. They combined their experience to start the school in 2010. They created a structure that helps students learn with confidence. They continue to guide the school and support every department.



Inspiration:

  • Mr. Mike Smith was motivated by the challenges he saw in local schools — overcrowded classrooms, lack of structured discipline, and limited focus on holistic development.
  • His vision was to create a school that not only teaches but also shapes character, where every child could thrive academically while learning values of responsibility and leadership.

  • Mrs. Diana Smith was driven by her experiences with young learners, noticing how early childhood education often lacked personal attention and nurturing environments.
  • Her dream was to establish a school where children feel safe, loved, and confident from their very first day and also ensure that the lower primary department remains a place of joy, curiosity, and growth.


Our History

Founders of Shine Mission School: Mr. Mike Smith and Mrs. Diana Smith.
The school began in 2016 when Mr. Mike and his wife decided to create a place that focused on strong learning and discipline. They started with a small class. They added more teachers as enrollment grew. Their goal was to give every child the chance to learn with confidence. They worked together to build a system that supports students. They kept the school open to new ideas. They improved the teaching methods every year. Their leadership shaped the culture of the school and set the standards that students follow today. The school now serves many families. It continues to grow because of the founders’ early commitment to quality and consistency. Their work still guides the direction of the school.

Growth and Expansion

  • As enrollment increased, more teachers joined the team, each bringing new energy and ideas. The school continuously adapts its teaching methods, blending discipline with innovative approaches. Growth is seen not just in numbers, but in the quality of learning experiences offered to students.Every year, Shine Mission School refines its system, ensuring students are supported academically and emotionally.
  • Expansion is treated as a step-by-step journey rather than a one-time milestone. New facilities, programs, and extracurricular activities are added gradually, responding to the needs of families and students. The founders keep the school open to fresh ideas, making improvement an ongoing cycle. This process of expansion ensures that Shine Mission School remains relevant, modern, and community-focused.

Present Day and Beyond

  • Today, Shine Mission School serves many families, but its growth is still in motion. The founders’ early commitment to quality and consistency continues to guide every decision.
  • The school’s culture of discipline, confidence, and creativity is not static — it evolves with each new generation of learners. Shine Mission School’s journey is ongoing, with the promise of future innovations and continued excellence.
