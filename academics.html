Academics at Shine Mission School

Shine Mission School delivers structured and disciplined academic instruction. Teaching focuses on clarity, consistency, and measurable progress. Lessons follow approved national standards. Teachers guide students closely to build confidence and strong study habits.

Academic Structure

The academic system is divided into clear learning stages. Each stage builds on the previous one. Students progress with steady assessment and guided support. This structure ensures no learner is left behind and high performers remain challenged.

Learning Levels

Kindergarten

Learning focuses on early reading, counting, communication, and behavior development. Activities support creativity, coordination, and classroom routines. The goal is readiness for formal learning.

Lower Primary

Students develop strong reading, writing, and numeracy skills. Subjects include English Language, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Creative Arts, and ICT basics. Practice and repetition strengthen understanding.

Upper Primary

Instruction becomes more detailed and analytical. Subjects include English Language, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, ICT, and Creative Arts. Students learn responsibility and exam preparation skills.

Junior High School

Teaching prepares students for national examinations. Core subjects include English Language, Mathematics, Integrated Science, Social Studies, ICT, and electives. Revision and assessment are consistent.

Assessment and Progress

Students are evaluated through classwork, homework, tests, and examinations. Continuous assessment helps teachers track performance and provide timely support. Reports are issued every term to keep parents informed.
