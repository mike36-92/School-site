Welcome to Shine Mission School

At Shine Mission School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. Our mission is to provide a supportive and stimulating environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and are committed to helping each student reach their full potential. Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and growth!

Our History

Founders of Shine Mission School: Mr. Mike Smith and Mrs. Diana Smith.
The school began in 2016 when Mr. Mike and his wife decided to create a place that focused on strong learning and discipline. They started with a small class. They added more teachers as enrollment grew. Their goal was to give every child the chance to learn with confidence. They worked together to build a system that supports students. They kept the school open to new ideas. They improved the teaching methods every year. Their leadership shaped the culture of the school and set the standards that students follow today. The school now serves many families. It continues to grow because of the founders’ early commitment to quality and consistency. Their work still guides the direction of the school.

Our Founders

Mr. Mike Smith || Mrs. Diana Smith

Mr. Mike Smith studied Education at the University of Cape Coast. He later trained in school administration and worked as a teacher for several years. He became a head teacher before starting the school. He focuses on discipline, planning, and strong academic standards.
His wife Mrs. Diana Smith studied Early Childhood Education at the University of Education, Winneba. She worked as a kindergarten teacher. She also trained other teachers in child development. She supports the lower primary department and manages student welfare. They combined their experience to start the school in 2010. They created a structure that helps students learn with confidence. They continue to guide the school and support every department.
